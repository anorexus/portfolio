Wusk

Wusk is a service that gives content creators such like youtube, souncloud instagram, vines, and blog. A new way to share their content with fans and collect contributions or donations. It also gives content creators a way to share exclusive content and experiences with their biggest fans. The idea of wusk come from ‘busking’ or street performance for gratitudes. We replace ‘B’ letter on ‘Busk’ and change it to ‘W’ on ‘Wusk’ that’s mean Web so the idea is busking on the web.

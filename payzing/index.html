Payzing

Payzing is a payments service for eCommerce and donation solutions. Built upon the Bitreserve platform (a cloud-based financial servers platform that makes sending money as easy, instant and free as email), Payzing enables merchants and consumers to exchange value instantly, securely, and for a fraction of the cost.
Payzing makes it easy for businesses to accept value online from customers anywhere in the world. Payzing is building infrastructre for the future. International expansion, for all methods of ecommerce and donations. Access to foreign currency payments and large currency conversion fees for customers had previously been one of the most difficult parts of expanding and ecommerce business overseas. Payzings solution is built in, by not only allowing the unbanked a means of exchanging value for products, services, or donations online via Bitreserve accounts, but doing so with 0% conversion fees.
